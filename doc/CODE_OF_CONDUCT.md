# Contributor Code of Conduct  

We are committed to fostering a welcoming and inclusive environment.  

---

## 👥 Our Pledge  

In the interest of building a positive community, we pledge to:  
- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.  
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.  
- Provide a harassment-free experience for everyone.  

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior  

- Use of inappropriate language or imagery.  
- Discrimination, harassment, or personal attacks.  
- Disruptive or disrespectful communication.

---

## 🌟 Our Responsibilities  

Project maintainers are responsible for:  
- Reviewing and managing issues and pull requests.  
- Removing or rejecting contributions that violate the Code of Conduct.  
- Addressing behavior that doesn’t align with this policy.

---

## 🛡️ Enforcement  

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in actions such as:  
- A warning or request to stop the behavior.  
- Temporary or permanent suspension from the project.  

---

## 📬 Reporting Issues  

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, report it to us via email:  
**[mansjobs@icloud.com](mailto:security@thinkingcoin.com)**  

---

Thank you for contributing to a positive and collaborative community!
